- One-pager Rural: top 20 ASCs covered **2,337 of 21,693 jobs (11%)** over 6 months

The drop is because:
1. **7 ASCs were handed off** to other managers (Gosman, Dejesus, etc.) — their claims no longer appear in Brown/Graham's data
2. **Different time windows** — one-pager was 6 months, current data is 14 weeks
3. **One-pager tracked top 20**, current slide focuses on top 10

The volume didn't disappear — it moved to other managers' books. That's the strategy working.
